Cyclone

For the vaal-themed counterpart, see vaal Cyclone.

Cyclone is a channeling attack skill that initiates a spinning attacks that rapidly hits nearby enemies. The player can move while channeling Cyclone.

Skill functions and interactions

Cyclone spins at the player's attack speed and deals area of effect damage twice per spin. When dual wielding, each weapon hits once per spin. While Cycloning, the player is immune to stun and knockback. Death, being frozen, and Bear Traps will still stop movement. Movement will also stop upon hitting an object. Characters can pass through enemies for the duration of the attack. Note that this is not considered Phasing.

Theoretical maximum Area of effect

The absolute maximum self-sustained range possible for Cyclone as of version 3.0.0 is 66. It can be achieved by a Deadeye dual wielding triple-white-socket Prismatic Eclipse s, wearing all the gear and jewels that increase area, weapon range, or flask effects (including the relevant corruptions), speccing into the relevant passive skills, linking lv21 q20% Cyclone, lv4 Empower, lv4 Enhance, lv21 IAoE together in an Item with a +1 to Level of Socketed Gems modifier and having Dying Sun active. The maximum range without using flasks is 80.

Item acquisition

This item is given as quest reward for the following quests: Sever the Right Hand in Act 3.
